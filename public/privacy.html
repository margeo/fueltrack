Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 5, 2026

FuelTrack ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our application at https://fueltrack.me.

1. Information We Collect

FuelTrack stores data locally on your device (via browser localStorage). We do not have servers that store your personal data.

If you choose to connect Google Fit, we access the following data from your Google account:

  • Step count
  • Distance traveled
  • Calories burned

This data is used solely to display your activity information within the app and is never stored on our servers or shared with third parties.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected to:

  • Display your daily fitness and nutrition data within the app
  • Calculate your calorie goals and nutritional targets
  • Provide AI-powered coaching recommendations

3. Data Storage

All personal data (profile information, food logs, exercise logs, weight logs) is stored locally on your device using browser localStorage. We do not transmit or store this data on external servers.

4. Third-Party Services

FuelTrack uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Fit API — to read fitness activity data (optional, requires your explicit authorization)
  • USDA FoodData Central API — to search for food nutritional information
  • Open Food Facts API — to search for food products
  • OpenRouter AI API — to provide AI coaching recommendations

Each of these services has its own privacy policy. We encourage you to review them.

5. Google API Scopes

When you connect Google Fit, FuelTrack requests access only to the following scopes:

  • fitness.activity.read — to read activity data (steps, calories)
  • fitness.location.read — to read distance data

We do not request write access to your Google Fit data. You can revoke this access at any time from your Google Account settings.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties. Google Fit data accessed through our app is used only within the app and is not stored or transmitted elsewhere.

7. Children's Privacy

FuelTrack is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Your Rights

You can clear all locally stored data at any time by clearing your browser's localStorage. To disconnect Google Fit, use the "Αποσύνδεση" button within the app or revoke access from your Google Account.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the date at the top of this page.

10. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: support@fueltrack.me
