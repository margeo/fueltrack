Logic

  1. No account — Use the app normally (food tracking, exercise, barcode), but no AI and no backup.
  2. Free account — Backup/sync + 20 trial AI requests (lifetime). Incentive: backup = safety.
  3. Paid $2.99/mo — Full AI Coach, 500 requests total/month, max 25/day. Need more? +500 for $2.99 extra.
  4. Demo — Unlimited AI, granted manually from admin panel.
500 requests = meal plans + training plans + grocery lists + questions + analysis. All together, one counter. Not 500 per type.
