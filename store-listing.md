## Full Description (English)

FuelTrack is a simple, powerful calorie and macro tracker designed to help you reach your fitness and nutrition goals.

**Key Features:**

- **Calorie & Macro Tracking** — Log your meals and track calories, protein, carbs, and fat with an easy-to-use interface
- **AI Food Photo Analysis** — Take a photo of your food and get instant nutritional estimates powered by AI
- **Barcode Scanner** — Scan product barcodes to instantly look up nutritional information from Open Food Facts and USDA databases
- **AI Coach** — Get personalized meal plans, training advice, and grocery lists tailored to your goals
- **Exercise Logging** — Track your workouts and see how they affect your daily calorie balance
- **Progress Dashboard** — View daily summaries, macro breakdowns, and track your progress over time
- **PDF Export** — Export your daily food log as a PDF for your nutritionist or personal records
- **Dark Mode** — Full dark mode support for comfortable tracking day and night
- **Bilingual** — Available in English and Greek

**Why FuelTrack?**

FuelTrack focuses on simplicity. No complicated menus, no overwhelming features. Just open the app, log your food, and stay on track. The AI features make it faster than ever — snap a photo or scan a barcode and you're done.

**Free to Use**

FuelTrack is free with generous daily limits. Upgrade to Pro for 500 AI-powered analyses per month.

**Privacy First**

Your data is yours. We don't sell it, we don't share it. See our full privacy policy at fueltrack.me/privacy.html.
